Logo

On the top left of the navigation bar of the homepage is a logo and the website name InkPlot.

The logo design journey started with a picture of a Korean Lady standing under a tree.

After exploring distinctive design techniques using affinity photo, the image was isolated and turned black.

This idea took inspiration from the iPod advertising art that has been globally iconic (Caplin, 2012).

GIF

On the top ribbon of the homepage is an animation used to instantly attract the userâ€™s attention. Initially this was an image of nine hands created by Johnson (2020) with no annotation.

The file was uploaded onto Figma and resized to a width of 1440 pixels and height of 224 pixels. Using a serif accessible font called Libre Baskerville (Stevenson, 2022) the slogan of story, setting and substance was added.

People with visual impairments should be able to read the words easily, so this font was chosen due to its elegant design clarity (LookScout, n.d.)

The files were uploaded onto GIFMaker.me (n.d.), to finally display a hand holding a quill that changes frame every 1700 milliseconds from story to setting, then lastly to substance before repeating again.
